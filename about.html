About EduRec
EduRec is an online portal made for NUS students to manage their education records. This includes financials, acadmeics, such as module registration, applying for graduation, and many other general administrative matters, such as paying for school fees.

To learn how to navigate EduRec, you may refer to our User Guide, linked down below. Alternatively, if you would like to contact EduRec with further queries, you may contact us at the link down below.
